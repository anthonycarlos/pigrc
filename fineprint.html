Limitations and Liabilities

A $50.00 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve your date. Upon confirmation (one week prior to your roast) you are liable for the number of guests confirmed. The roast can be held rain or shine. To maintain our cost, we try to stay within reasonable driving distance. However your “long distance roast” could be considered if you are willing to order a minimum of 60 servings and pay travel expenses of $50.00

Our roasting staff does look forward to gratuities for a job well done.
